#meta 
The Gaian Calendar has been adopted as the standard calendar for the Gaia Atmos, the skies post [[1.Notes/Upheaval\|Upheaval]].

There is 365 days to a year, 366 every 4 years.
12 Months in a year.
7 Days in a week.
